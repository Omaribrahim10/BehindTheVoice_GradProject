ABOUT US

Who are we

We are a team of computer science graduates specializing in speech emotion recognition. Our project combines cutting-edge techniques such as Diarization, transcription, and emotion detection using CNN and LSTM models to understand the emotional nuances in speech.

Our story

Our journey began with a shared fascination for the intricate relationship between speech and emotion. Recognizing the profound impact that emotional understanding can have on human-computer interaction, we embarked on this project to create a system that can interpret and respond to human emotions through voice. Over months of rigorous research, experimentation, and collaboration, we developed "Behind the Voice," a platform that bridges the gap between technology and human emotion, offering a new dimension to interaction and empathy in the digital world.

Our mission

Our mission is to revolutionize how technology perceives and interacts with human emotions. By advancing speech emotion recognition, we aim to create systems that can empathetically respond to users, enhancing communication, accessibility, and emotional well-being. We believe that by enabling technology to understand and respond to human emotions, we can foster more meaningful and supportive interactions.

Why We Chose "Behind the Voice"  

We chose the name "Behind the Voice" to emphasize the deeper layers of meaning and emotion embedded in speech that often go unnoticed. This name reflects our commitment to uncovering and understanding the emotional context that lies beyond mere words. By focusing on the subtleties "behind the voice," we highlight our project's goal to capture and interpret the emotional undertones of speech, offering a more comprehensive and empathetic technological interaction.

BEHIND THE VOICE

Our goal is to use machine learning to predict emotions in phone conversations, especially in call centers.
