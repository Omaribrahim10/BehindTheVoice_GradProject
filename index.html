FEATURES

Speaker Diarization

Dividing Audio Streams by Speaker Identity

Speaker diarisation (or diarization) is the process of partitioning an audio stream containing human speech into homogeneous segments according to the identity of each speaker.

Improving Transcription Clarity with Speaker Turn Structuring

It can enhance the readability of an automatic speech transcription by structuring the audio stream into speaker turns.

The Role of Speaker Segmentation and Clustering in Diarization

It is used to answer the question "who spoke when?" Speaker diarisation is a combination of speaker segmentation and speaker clustering.

Transcription

Transcription: Turning Speech into Text

Transcription changes spoken words into text, making it easier to keep track of audio content.

Fast and Accurate Transcription Tools

Automatic transcription tools use smart technology to quickly and accurately turn audio into text.

Why Transcription Matters in Media, Law, and Research

Transcriptions are important for keeping precise records in journalism, legal work, and research.

Emotion Detection

Emotion Detection with CNN and LSTM

Emotion detection finds feelings in text or speech using CNN and LSTM models, which act like the brain.

Better Emotion Detection with CNNs and LSTMs

CNNs and LSTMs help detect emotions by looking at patterns and context.

How CNN and LSTM Help in Finding Emotions

Emotion detection helps in understanding user feelings in areas like customer service, social media, and mental health.

Sentiment Analysis

Understanding Emotions in Text Through Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis is the process of detecting and interpreting the emotional tone within a piece of text. It helps in determining whether the text expresses positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.

Applications of Sentiment Analysis in Business and Social Media

This technique is widely used in business to gauge customer opinions, improve products, and enhance customer satisfaction. It's also vital in social media to analyze public sentiment and trends.
